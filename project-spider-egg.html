Spider Egg

The SpiderEgg is a modular, autonomous unmanned underwater vehicle designed for ISR, strike, and mine countermeasure missions through distributed lethality. It details a mission-flexible payload architecture capable of deploying kinetic “Spiders,” advanced sensors, FPV drones, and electronic warfare or relay systems across undersea, surface, and air domains. The platform emphasizes stealth and survivability through quiet electric propulsion, multi-layered PACE communications, and rapid surface exposure enabled by thermal and EO sensing. Built with an open, platform-agnostic architecture, SpiderEgg integrates seamlessly with submarines, surface combatants, and large UUVs to provide scalable mission or hard-kill effects from a single system
