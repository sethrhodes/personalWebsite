Search & Rescue AR

In disaster zones and rural search-and-rescue missions, responders must manage radios, maps, thermal cameras, and medical tools in low visibility and high noise environments. Search&Rescue AR is a zero-latency AR system that overlays thermal target detection (bounding boxes), patient vitals, and GPS/Wi-Fi positioning directly into a passthrough HUD—allowing rescuers to see people, health data, and location without taking their hands off the mission. The system combines an AR passthrough HUD on XREAL Air Pro 2 glasses with a real-time thermal imaging pipeline that detects heat signatures and streams bounding boxes to a custom app. This enables true zero-latency passthrough viewing, with patient vitals from a PPG sensor integrated into the HUD widget system.
